# Mulakupetti Website - Modern Design Update

## Overview
Your website has been completely transformed with a modern, professional design inspired by the reference website (mulakupetti.com). The updated design features clean layouts, premium typography, smooth animations, and an optimized user experience.

## Key Changes Made

### 1. **Home Page Enhancements** (`src/app/page.js`)
- ✅ **Hero Section**: Immersive full-screen hero with gradient overlay and call-to-action buttons
- ✅ **Featured Products Grid**: Modern 4-column product showcase with hover effects
- ✅ **Category Section**: Eye-catching category cards (Spices, Dry Fruits, Honey)
- ✅ **Why Choose Us Section**: 6-column feature grid highlighting key benefits:
  - Quality Assurance
  - Efficient Transport
  - 100% Natural
  - Certified Quality
  - Trusted Partnership
  - Easy Ordering
- ✅ **Brand Story Section**: Compelling narrative about the company journey
- ✅ **Signature Collections**: Editorial grid showcasing featured products

### 2. **Shop Page Redesign** (`src/app/shop/page.js`)
- ✅ **Hero Section**: Gradient background with compelling headline
- ✅ **Product Grid**: Clean 4-column layout with 8 products
- ✅ **Product Cards**: 
  - Sale badges
  - Price comparison (original vs. current)
  - Smooth hover animations
  - Add to Cart buttons
- ✅ **Sorting Options**: Dropdown menu for sorting by latest, price, or bestsellers
- ✅ **CTA Section**: Call-to-action section encouraging users to continue shopping

### 3. **Navigation Bar** (`src/components/Navbar.jsx`)
- ✅ **Glassmorphism**: Modern frosted glass effect on navbar
- ✅ **Navigation Links**: Clean menu with Collection, Our Story, Sourcing, Contact
- ✅ **Responsive Design**: Mobile hamburger menu for smaller screens
- ✅ **Contact Display**: Phone number on desktop
- ✅ **Cart Button**: Premium styled cart button

### 4. **Footer Component** (`src/components/Footer.jsx`)
- ✅ **Brand Section**: Company intro with social media links
- ✅ **Quick Links**: Shop, Our Story, About, Contact
- ✅ **Support Links**: FAQ, Shipping, Returns, Privacy Policy
- ✅ **Contact Info**: Address, phone, email
- ✅ **Social Media**: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube
- ✅ **Footer Navigation**: Terms, Privacy Policy

### 5. **CSS Styling Enhancements** (`src/app/globals.css`)
Added modern styling for:
- `.product-card`: Product cards with shadow and hover effects
- `.category-card`: Category showcase cards
- `.feature-card`: Feature/benefit cards
- Color utilities: `bg-forest`, `bg-emerald`, `text-forest`, `text-emerald`, `bg-gray-50`
- Smooth transitions and animations throughout

### 6. **Layout Updates** (`src/app/layout.js`)
- ✅ Added Footer component to all pages
- ✅ Maintained metadata for SEO

## Design Features

### Modern Elements
- **Luxury Color Palette**: Forest green, emerald, sage, gold accents
- **Typography**: Playfair Display for headings, Outfit for body
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects on navbar and cards
- **Smooth Animations**: Hover effects, transitions, and scroll animations
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop

### User Experience
- Product cards with hover zoom effects
- Smooth color transitions on interactions

- Easy-to-use category filters
- Professional footer with complete information
